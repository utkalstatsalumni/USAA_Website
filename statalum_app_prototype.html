PG Department of Statistics, Utkal University

Established in 1965, the department has been a premier centre for statistical education in Odisha, producing alumni who serve across academia, industry, and government.

Our Mission

To foster lifelong bonds among alumni, support academic excellence, facilitate career growth, and contribute to the advancement of the statistical sciences through collaboration and shared knowledge.

Association Founded

The Utkal Statistics Alumni Association was formally constituted in 2005 with a charter of 40 founding members.
